== Astra
- Satan: 			Cleanse Tag (10% chance to despawn any non-boss enemy at the start of the room) 
					"Repels wild creatures"

- Lamb:				Soothe Bell (if not at full health, any consumable being spawned has a 8% chance to spawn a heart along it) 
					"The chime comforts you" 
					
- Ultra Greedier: 	Resolve of Purity (spawned/granted/current black hearts are turned into 2 soul hearts each) 
					"Let your soul not be swayed"
					
- All Hard Mode:	Life Orb (big damage up, you take double damage)
					"Hit hard, get hit hard"

== Tainted Astra
- Four Bosses:		Air Balloon (grants Flying, disappears when hit) 					
					"Don't get hit" 
					
- Mother:			Choice Scarf (speed up, doesn't come off, auto drops at the beginning of the next floor) 
					"You feel fixated"

					"Something Mega"

					"Discard your hand and draw 7"

=== Items:
== Astra
- Delirium:			Confusion (charged aoe blast + enemy slowdown, Astra's starting item)
					"Brainpower!"

- Boss Rush:		Blink (teleport around the room, Astra's starting item)
					"Once here, once there"

- Isaac:			Psychic (passive effect, every few seconds a nearby enemy is picked into air and thrown away, dealing damage to it)
					"Stay back!"
				
- ???:				Future Sight (delayed Doom Desire explosion on where you stood)
					"Delayed blast"

- Hush:				Triple Axel (burst of freezing tears)
					"Your legs are too short for this"
					
- Mother: 			Wish (full heal + overheal on start of next floor)
					"Hold on for a bit longer"

- Ultra Greed:		Tapu's Fin (chance to fire a Moonblast tear that zaps everything in the room and focuses on bosses)
					"Goes well with Primeape"

- The Beast:		Witch's Braids (Hatterene hair familiar that automatically attacks in melee around the user)
					"Bloodless, and yet still full of hatred"				
					
- Mega Satan:		Hypno's Pendulum (Familiar that can be swung around the player like Guppy's hairball- deals little damage, but can slow, stun, and even charm enemies it hits)
					"Also works as a bludgeon"
		
- Mom's Heart:		Moon Baby (Ralts familiar, passively repels tears and occasionally knocks all nearby enemies back)
					"Psychic/Fairy friend"
	

== Tainted Astra
- Delirium:			Doom Desire (Epic Fetus style tear explosion, TAstra's starting item)
					"Wish of Destruction"

- The Beast:		Latias' Mask (flight, camo undies effect, far away enemies are confused, wafer)
					"Torn off of its previous owner"

- Mega Satan:		Heart Fin (shot speed down, damage up, tears deal damage to enemies when passing by them)
					"Painful to be near"
					
- Other:			Frustration (teleports to a random spot in the room, TAstra's starting item, not unlockable)
					"Powerless, aimless"
